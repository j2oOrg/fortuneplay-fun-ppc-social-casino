The TopzLots code of conduct.

Every guest deserves a calm, creative lounge. These guidelines outline the behavior we celebrate and the lines we enforce.

Our values

TopzLots is built on curiosity, empathy, and a zero-harassment stance. Hold space for different cultures, use inclusive language, and honor quiet modes when players need a break.

We reserve the right to remove any content or account that contradicts these values even if it is not explicitly mentioned below.

Harassment & abuse (not allowed)

  • Bullying, threats, or targeted harassment.
  • Unwanted romantic or sexual messages.
  • Doxxing or sharing private information.

Hate speech & discrimination

  • Slurs against protected characteristics.
  • Extremist propaganda, coded language, or symbols.
  • Encouraging exclusion, segregation, or violence.

Spam & exploitation

  • Commercial solicitations or referral schemes.
  • Malware, phishing, or deceptive links.
  • Impersonating staff, moderators, or other players.

Responsible play

Share resources, not pressure. Encouraging unhealthy behavior, bypassing limits, or distributing unverified tips is prohibited.

Reporting workflow

  1. Use the in-room report tool or email support@TopzLots Online.
  2. Attach screenshots or timestamps if available.
  3. We acknowledge within 24 hours and update you after review.

Enforcement ladder

  • Friendly reminder → for accidental slips.
  • Official warning → logged on your profile.
  • Suspension → 24 hours to permanent depending on severity.
  • Removal → immediate for hate, threats, or exploitation.

Appeals

Believe we made a mistake? Email legal@TopzLots Online within 14 days and include the username, date, and reason. A human will review within 10 days.
